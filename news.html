27 May 2026 · Orange NSW

Duntryleague Golf Club, Orange

Roof leak repairs and ceiling replacement above the cafe and stairwell of a heritage clubhouse, with roof sections more than 100 years old and original shingle tiles under the sheeting.

We designed and installed a counter-levered access solution so almost no weight sat on the fragile roof. Outside, roof access scaffold and temporary handrails got the crew safely to a skylight and chimney above the undercover cafe seating.

8 July 2022 · Goulburn NSW

St Peters & Pauls Cathedral

Built between 1871 and 1890, the State Heritage listed bluestone cathedral is undergoing major restoration: a new fleche, stone rejuvenation, structural securing and underpinning, and a full slate roof replacement.

Our scaffold supports the works inside and out.

12 December 2022 · Crookwell NSW

Have you considered a temporary roof structure?

Two years of wild weather and constant rain made it very difficult to program and get works done.

This Kedar temporary roof at the Crookwell wind farm meant technicians from interstate could get in and finish the job, rain, hail or shine.
