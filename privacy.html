What information we collect

We keep data collection to a minimum:

  • Enquiries: If you contact us through the “Get a quote” form or the “Apply by email” links, your browser opens your own email program with the details you entered. No enquiry data is stored on or submitted to this website’s server. Your message reaches us only when you choose to send the email. When you email or call us, we receive the information you provide (such as your name, phone number, email address and details about your job) and use it solely to respond to your request.
  • Server logs: Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically record technical information such as your IP address, browser type, date and time of access and the pages requested. This is used for security and to operate the site, and is retained only for as long as necessary for those purposes.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not set cookies and does not use analytics, advertising or third-party tracking services. Fonts are served from our own server, so no font data is shared with third parties.

Operator note (remove before publishing): if any third-party service is added before go-live (e.g. an external image/content delivery network, a map embed, analytics or a hosted contact-form service), it must be listed here together with the data it receives.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes;
  • To process and respond to job applications;
  • To operate, secure and maintain this website;
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it for automated decision-making or profiling.

Disclosure

We may disclose personal information to service providers who help us operate (for example, our website hosting and email providers), and where required or authorised by law. We do not otherwise disclose your information to third parties without your consent.

Data security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law.

Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the details above.

If you are located in the EU or Switzerland, you may also have the right to request erasure or restriction of your data, to object to processing, and to data portability. The legal basis for processing enquiry data is the steps taken at your request prior to, or in connection with, a contract; server logs are processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in operating a secure website.

Complaints

If you have a privacy concern, please contact us first so we can address it. You may also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au. Visitors in the EU/Switzerland may contact their local data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available on this page, with the date of the last update shown above.
