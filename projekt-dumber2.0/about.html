About

Who we are

Dunbar Scaffold Hire has spent more than 25 years in scaffold, formwork and safety. The crew covers advanced scaffolders, estimators, engineers, heavy equipment operators, crane dogmen, confined space personnel and EWP operators.

We run Layher scaffolding, internationally recognised as the leading scaffold system for mining, construction, infrastructure, petrochemical and event work. That expertise in large-scale, complex jobs is what built our reputation as one of Australia's leading providers of complete scaffolding solutions.

How We Work

How we run jobs

Health & Safety

Reviewed yearly

Safe, healthy work for crews, contractors and the public. Hazards are eliminated or minimised as far as reasonably practicable, and senior management reviews the safety system every year. The goal is zero injuries.

Quality

Aligned with AS/NZS ISO 9001

Services that meet agreed client requirements and often exceed them, controlled through a systematic quality management approach with trained personnel behind it.

Environment

Aligned with AS/NZS ISO 14001

Sustainable development principles applied across the whole construction process, with performance targets set each year and pollution prevention built into how we work.
